Dengue Fever Outbreak in Krishnapur District

Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, India

HighH

A significant increase in dengue fever cases has been reported in Krishnapur District. Residents are advised to take immediate preventive measures, including eliminating stagnant water sources and using mosquito repellents. Seek medical attention if symptoms like high fever, severe headache, or joint pain appear.

Water Contamination Advisory for Rampur Village

District Health Department, Rampur

MediumM

Due to recent heavy rainfall and suspected contamination, residents of Rampur Village are advised to boil all drinking water for at least one minute before consumption. Use filtered water for cooking and personal hygiene. Report any instances of waterborne illness to the local health center immediately.

Oct 25, 2024
Rampur Village, Bihar
